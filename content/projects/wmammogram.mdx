## Overview

wMammogram is an interactive educational web course designed to increase awareness about mammograms and breast cancer screening. The project was developed through Grady Development and has had measurable impact on healthcare outcomes.

## Impact

The course substantially increased the number of mammograms performed in the target population, demonstrating the power of well-designed educational technology in healthcare settings.

## Technical Implementation

The application was built with a focus on accessibility and user engagement, ensuring that the educational content was easy to understand and navigate for users of all technical skill levels.

### Key Features

- **Interactive Modules**: Engaging content with quizzes and progress tracking
- **Accessibility First**: WCAG-compliant design for maximum reach
- **Mobile Responsive**: Optimized experience across all devices
- **Analytics**: Tracking engagement and completion rates for continuous improvement

## Lessons Learned

This project reinforced the importance of:

- Building accessible applications that work for everyone
- Collaborating with domain experts (healthcare professionals)
- Measuring real-world impact of software solutions
- Optimizing for user engagement in educational contexts
